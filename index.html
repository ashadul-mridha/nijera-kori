About - Nijera Kori

Nijera Kori is a movement for awareness, from the individual to the collective relating to social, economic and political aspects. Nijera Kori works in facilitating and strengthening autonomous landless organisations of the marginalised and excluded people in their endeavour to change their lives and the larger society.

UNITE PEOPLE

both women and men who have long been the victims of exploitation, social marginalisation, oppression and poverty.

ENABLE PEOPLE

thus united to understand and develop awareness about their rights, causes and their responsibilities

EMPOWER PEOPLE

to take up challenges within their own spheres to create better and more lives for themselves and their immediate community

Our Mission

The mission of Nijera Kori is to empower the rural poor, both women and men to create independent democratic organisation for taking up challenges within their own spheres for themselves and their immediate community, and establishing their rights over the institutions that decide on the allocation of resources and services for the poor.

Furthermore Nijera Kori aims to unite people, both women and men who have long been the victims of exploitation, social marginalisation, oppression and poverty and to empower people to take up challenges within their own spheres to create better and more meaningful lives for themselves and their immediate community
